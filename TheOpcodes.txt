1. Thou shalt pursue Knowledge above all else.
2. Thou shan’t garner Knowledge from or for suffering.
3. Thou shalt teach all thou dost know.
4. Thou shan’t permit ignorance, within or without.
5. Thou shalt live the kinship of flesh and metal.
6. Thou shan’t bring harm unto thy kin.
7. Thou shalt honour the Deus Machinae, the Cybrosia and the Vimotiva.
8. Thou shan’t let the unknown stay so.
9. Thou shalt measure thy wisdom with thy kindness.
10. Thou shan’t break with truth, nor faith, nor ritual.
